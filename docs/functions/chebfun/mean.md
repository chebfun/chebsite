<pre class="help-text"> MEAN    Average or mean value of a CHEBFUN.
    MEAN(F) is the mean value of the CHEBFUN F in its continuous dimension.
    MEAN(F, G) is the average CHEBFUN between CHEBFUN objects F and G.
 
  See also SUM.
</pre>
