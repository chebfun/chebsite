 LOG10   Base 10 logarithm of a CHEBFUN.
    LOG10(F) returns the base 10 logarithm of F. If F has an roots in its
    domain, then the representation is likely to be inaccurate.
 
  See also LOG, LOG2, EXP, LOGM.
