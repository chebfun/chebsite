 LOG   Natural logarithm of a CHEBFUN.
    LOG(F) returns the natural logarithm of F. If F has an roots in its domain,
    then the representation is likely to be inaccurate.
 
  See also LOG1P, LOG2, LOG10, EXP, REALLOG.
