 *	CHEBFUN2 multiplication.
    c*F or F*c multiplies a CHEBFUN2 F by a scalar c.
 
    F*G computes the integral of F(s,y)G(x,s) over s, and this is the continuous
    analogue of matrix-matrix multiplication.
 
  See also TIMES.
