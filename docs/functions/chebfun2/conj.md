<pre class="help-text"> CONJ   Complex conjugate of a CHEBFUN2.
    CONJ(F) returns the complex conjugate of F.  For a complex F, CONJ(F) =
    REAL(F) - i*IMAG(F).
 
  See also REAL, IMAG. 
</pre>
